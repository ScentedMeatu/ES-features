------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Array.prototype.flat()

used to flatten array with depth.
let arr = [1,[2,[3,4],5],6];
arr.flat(2); //[1,2,3,4,5,6]
2 is the depth.
arr.flat(); //[1,2,[3,4],5,6]
